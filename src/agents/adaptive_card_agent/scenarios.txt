# Adaptive Card Agent - Sample Scenarios
# Use these queries to test the specific card templates

## 1. Hero Card (Welcome/Introduction)
- Hi there! Who are you?
- Welcome me to the system.
- Introduce yourself.

## 2. Alert Card (Status/Warning)
- System is overheating!
- CRITICAL: The server is on fire!
- Alert: Low disk space detected.
- Status update for module X: Failed.

## 3. Data Summary Card (Charts/Stats)
- Show me the sales report for Q1.
- Display the weekly user growth stats.
- What are the latest performance metrics?

## 4. Form Card (Inputs)
- I need to submit some feedback.
- Open the feedback form.
- Let me report an issue.

## 5. List Card (Tasks/Items)
- What are my top 3 tasks for today?
- Show me my pending items.
- List my priority actions.

## 6. Simple Card (Text)
- Say hello world.
- Just print a simple message.
- Echo: Hello.

## 7. Flight Update Card
- Is flight UA123 on time?
- What is the status of flight AA999?
- Check flight DL456.

## 8. Weather Card
- What is the weather like in Seattle?
- Forecast for New York City.
- Is it raining in London?

## 9. Stock Update Card
- Show me the stock price for GOOGL.
- How is MSFT doing today?
- Quote for AAPL.

## 10. Calendar Invite Card
- Create a calendar invite for the team sync at 10am tomorrow.
- Schedule a meeting with the design team.
- Invite Alok to a project review.

## 11. Restaurant Details Card
- Recommend a good sushi restaurant nearby.
- Find me an Italian place for dinner.
- Details for the top rated burger joint.

## 12. Popup Action Card (Tools)
- Open the calendar for me.
- Open the city selector.
- I need to select a location.
- Show me the checklist.
